🌙 Bedtime Routine

⏰ Goal: 7:45–8pm in bed. Start at 7pm on non-bath nights, 6:40pm on bath nights. Weekend? A little later is totally fine!
  1. Brush teeth, wash face & hands (washcloths in linen closet), use the bathroom
  2. Put on PJs — Lila likes to pick her own!
  3. Lila: Put on a pull-up. Use Aquaphor liberally (it's there if needed). Use a baby wipe on her before bed and in the morning to keep things fresh. 🧴 Wipes are in each bathroom — don't flush them!
  4. Water bottles to bed — Asher on his nightstand, Lila in the corner of her crib.
  5. 📚 Read ~2 books: each kid picks one. Read all together in Asher's bed, your bed, or the guest bed — all good!

💡 Nightlights

  • Both kids have colored lights — auto-turn blue at 6pm and change colors through the night
  • Lila's room has an egg-shaped reading light — tap twice on/off, long-hold to adjust brightness. Leave it OFF overnight

  • Lila's room: keep the second light switch ON — this powers the white noise + baby monitor

  • Turn on the white noise machine in the hall too
